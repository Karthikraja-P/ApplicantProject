Behavioural Games Assessment

3 games · 5 minutes each · 15 minutes total

🎈 Game 1 — Balloon Game (BART)
Measures: Risk Appetite vs. Reward
Pump a balloon to earn money — but it may pop. Bank your earnings before it bursts. Find the optimal pump point based on probability.
🃏 Game 2 — Cards Game (Iowa Gambling Task)
Measures: Pattern Recognition & Expected Value (EV)
Draw cards from 4 decks. Some decks have hidden large losses. Identify the high-value decks and maximise your balance.
🧠 Game 3 — Hard/Easy Task
Measures: Effort-Based Decision Making
Each round choose an Easy task ($1) or a Hard task ($3), then answer a maths question. Correct answers earn the reward.
Tips
  • Each game has its own 5-minute timer — manage within each window
  • There are no right or wrong personalities — play naturally
  • Results measure cognitive style, not just score
