## Question 23
**Title:** John needs 13 bottles of water from the store. John can only carry 3 at a time. What is the minimum number of trips John needs to make?  
**Options:**  
- A: 3  
- B: 4  
- C: 4 1/2  
- D: 5  
- E: 6  
**Answer:** D (3)  
**Solving Logic:** 13 divided by 3 is 4 remainder 1. So 4 trips is not enough; he needs a 5th trip. Answer: 5.
